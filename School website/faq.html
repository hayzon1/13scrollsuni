Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions for 13 Scrolls, we are ready to help

How can I apply to 13 Scrolls University?

You can apply to 13 Scrolls University by completing the online application form available on our official website. Make sure to submit all required documents and meet the application deadlines.

Are there any scholarships or financial aid options available at 13 Scrolls University?

Yes, 13 Scrolls University offers various scholarships and financial aid options to eligible students. These include merit-based scholarships, need-based grants, and work-study programs. We encourage you to explore the financial aid opportunities on our website and submit the necessary applications.

What are the admission requirements for 13 Scrolls University?

The admission requirements for 13 Scrolls University may vary depending on the program you are applying to. Generally, you will need to submit your high school transcripts, standardized test scores (such as SAT or ACT), letters of recommendation, and a personal statement.

How much is the tuition fee at 13 Scrolls University?

The tuition fee at 13 Scrolls University can vary depending on the program and the student's residency status. We recommend visiting our website or contacting the admissions office for the most up-to-date information on tuition fees.

What majors or programs does 13 Scrolls University offer?

13 Scrolls University offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs across various disciplines. Some popular majors include Business Administration, Computer Science, Psychology, Engineering, and Fine Arts. You can find a complete list of programs on our website.

Does 13 Scrolls University have on-campus housing options?

Yes, 13 Scrolls University provides on-campus housing facilities for students. Our residence halls offer a comfortable and convenient living environment with various amenities. However, availability is limited, so we recommend applying for housing early.

Can I transfer credits from another university to 13 Scrolls University?

Yes, 13 Scrolls University accepts transfer credits from other accredited institutions. The transfer credit evaluation process will assess the compatibility of your previous coursework with our curriculum. You will need to submit official transcripts and syllabi for evaluation.

Does 13 Scrolls University offer study abroad programs?

Yes, 13 Scrolls University encourages students to participate in study abroad programs to gain international exposure and cultural experiences. We have partnerships with universities around the world, offering various exchange programs and study abroad opportunities.

Are there opportunities for research or internships at 13 Scrolls University?

Absolutely! 13 Scrolls University promotes research and internship opportunities for students. We have well-equipped research facilities and collaborate with industry partners to provide valuable experiential learning experiences.

What support services are available for students at 13 Scrolls University?

13 Scrolls University offers a range of support services to ensure student success. These include academic advising, career counseling, tutoring services, health and wellness resources, and student organizations for extracurricular involvement.
